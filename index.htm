My Favorite Trips and Sites

My favorite out of every trip I've been on was to Italy, pictured here around the text. I went in Feruary
of this year (2025) for about 2 weeks. It was a school trip so it was mostly historical sites and museums,
but I enjoy that stuff so it was really fun!

  • My favorite museum that we visited was The Vatican Museums.
  • We stayed in both Rome (left) and Sorrento (right) which is is Naples.
  • My favorite site that we visited was Pompeii and Herculaneum, both destroyed by Vesuvius.
  • One of the best parts of the trip was the food from the streets and cafes like the pizza, coffee, and rice balls.
  • It was a school trip, so I had about 25 friends there with me which made it really fun.

This is my trip to Disney World that I went on in January of this year. I also went on this trip with my school, to play with my school band and chorus. Lastly in my top 3 is my trip to Mexico that I went on with my family in
June of 2022. We stayed in a super nice hotel and the weather was
amazing the entire time. While we were there, we went to Tulum, ruins of
the Mayan Civilization. We were there for about 10 days. The beaches and
jungles we saw on excursions were some of the most beautiful I've ever seen.
The food we had here outside of the hotel was some of the best I've ever had.
Also, the culture was amazing, the locals were so inviting and very fun to talk
to, expecially in Spanish. I took Spanish throughout highschool, so this trip was
a great test and I actually did better than I thought I would have. This trip
remains a top 3 because of the food and the culture and the beauty of course.
